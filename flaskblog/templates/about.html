  • African Developers Forum is simply a platform where developers from across the African continent share, advise, and debate about their development experiences and the issues they have faced or still face. The aim of this platform is to empower and equip developers with the right information and with the right tools to build a better Africa. We believe Africa has the solutions to its problems, we just need the platform and opportunities to showcase our skills and ideas.

  • Frequently Asked Questions

    How to Register ?
    1. Click on Register
    2. In the register page, fill in a username, email address, and your password
    3. Click Sign Up
    4. You would then have successfully registered at African Devs Forum :)
    How to write a Post ?
    1. Once logged in, you will see a New Post button on the navigation bar
    2. Click New Post
    3. Write the Title and Content of your post.
    4. Click on Post
    5. You would then have successfully posted an article.
    How to edit your Profile ?
    1. Once logged in, you will see an Account button on the navigation bar
    2. Click on Account
    3. You can edit your Username and Email address.
    4. Click on Update when you are done
    5. You would then have successfully updated at your details.
    Forgot your Password ?
    1. On the log in page, click on Forgot Password.
    2. On the Reset page, enter the email address you registered with and click on the Request Password Reset button.
    3. You will receive an email with a reset link. Click on the link.
    4. You will be taken to a Reset Password page, enter your new password and click on the Reset Password button.
    5. Your password will then be reset and now you can login with your new password.
    View all posts of another user ?
    1. To view posts by another user, simply click on their username above their post.
    Edit your own Post ?
    1. Once logged in, you can edit your post by clicking on its title.
    2. You will have the option to Update or Delete your post.
    3. To Update, click on the Update button.
    4. In the Update Post page, edit your post and click on Post to update.
    5. To Delete, simply click on the Delete Button
